# Paper PDFs — Teaching Use Notice

The PDF files under `resources/papers/` are bundled with this repository solely
for **non-commercial educational use** as part of the RecSys Atlas tutorial.

## Ownership and licensing

Each paper remains the property of its authors and/or the original publisher
(ACM, IEEE, arXiv, the corporate research lab, etc.). The arXiv-hosted papers
are redistributed under arXiv's non-exclusive license; the publisher-hosted
papers (ACM/IEEE and corporate mirrors) are included here for convenience so
that the tutorial's in-site paper reader can display the exact passages the
notes refer to.

## Acceptable use

- Reading alongside the tutorial and following the embedded evidence anchors.
- Personal study and classroom teaching.

## If you are a rights holder

If you are an author or rights holder of any included paper and would like it
removed from this repository, please open an issue or contact the maintainer
and it will be taken down promptly. The annotated highlights and sticky-note
comments are added programmatically by `scripts/annotate_papers.py` and are not
part of the original publications.

## No warranty

The papers are provided "as is" without warranty. The tutorial's annotations
and interpretations reflect this project's reading and may contain errors; they
do not represent the positions of the original authors.
